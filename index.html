2023 International Conference

“Current Trends and Achievements in Life Sciences”

Registration deadline is 24th of October 2023!

Date:

25-27th of October, 2023

Organizer:

Shenzhen MSU-BIT University

 

Mission

The III International Conference “Current Trends and Achievements in Life Sciences” is committed to fostering collaboration, innovation, and the exchange of knowledge among scientists, researchers and educators. Our mission is to create a dynamic and inclusive platform that showcases the latest developments in Structural Biology and Biomedicine, Biodiversity and Bioresources, and Ecology, with the ultimate aim of advancing our understanding of life and its myriad complexities.

 

Conference Objectives

Promote Cutting-Edge Research: We aim to provide a forum where researchers and scientists can present and discuss their pioneering work in Structural Biology and Biomedicine. By showcasing the latest breakthroughs in these fields, we hope to inspire further innovation and progress.

Celebrate Biodiversity and Bioresources: Biodiversity is the cornerstone of life on Earth. In the Biodiversity and Bioresources section, our mission is to highlight the importance of preserving our planet’s rich biological diversity while exploring the sustainable utilization of bioresources for the benefit of humanity.

Advance Ecological Understanding: Ecology is fundamental to our understanding of the interrelationships between organisms and their environments. In the Ecology section, we strive to promote research that addresses critical environmental challenges, encourages sustainable practices, and fosters a deeper appreciation for the natural world.

Facilitate Multidisciplinary Collaboration: We believe that breakthroughs often occur at the intersection of disciplines. Our mission is to facilitate multidisciplinary collaboration by bringing together experts from various fields within Life Sciences, encouraging the exchange of ideas, methods, and perspectives.

Inspire the Next Generation: We are committed to nurturing the passion for Life Sciences in the emerging generation of researchers and students. Through educational sessions and workshops, we aim to inspire and prepare future leaders in these fields.

Global Outreach: Our conference embraces diversity and welcomes participants from around the world. We encourage a global perspective on the challenges and opportunities in Life Sciences, recognizing that solutions to complex issues often require international cooperation.

 

Join Us in Exploring the Wonders of Life Sciences

We invite scientists, researchers, students and educators to join us in the III International Conference “Current Trends and Achievements in Life Sciences.” By participating, you become part of a vibrant community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge, preserving biodiversity, and finding sustainable solutions for a healthier planet.

Together, we can unravel the mysteries of life, celebrate its diversity, and work towards a more harmonious coexistence with the natural world.

Student Presentation & Poster Awards
Three best presentation awards and three best poster awards will be given to students at the conference. The winners will be selected by a panel of judges and will be recognized at the close ceremony of the conferences. Certificates and gifts will be presented to the winners.
